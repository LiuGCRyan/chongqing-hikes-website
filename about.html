HI, I'M RYAN

Born and raised in Chongqing, I've spent my entire life exploring this city — from the tourist hotspots to the hidden trails that even locals don't know about.

Before becoming a guide, I worked in international trade, where I learned to communicate effectively with people from all over the world. That experience taught me how to bridge cultures, explain complex things simply, and make people feel welcome.

Now, I combine my local knowledge with my English skills to show foreigners the real Chongqing — not just the Instagram spots, but the places that make this city special to me.

WHY I DO THIS

I love seeing people's reactions when they experience Chongqing for the first time. The "wow" moment when they see the 8D city from above. The surprise when they taste real hotpot in an air-raid shelter. The quiet wonder on a mountain trail at sunrise.

China is opening up, and more foreigners are coming. I want to be the one who shows them my home — the way I see it.
