FREQUENTLY ASKED

How do I pay?

I accept PayPal for international clients, WeChat Pay / Alipay if you're already in China. A 20% deposit secures your date; remaining balance can be paid on the day.

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before. 50% refund if 24-48 hours. No refund for less than 24 hours notice (I can try to reschedule instead).

Do you pick up from hotels?

Yes! For city tours, I'll meet you at your hotel lobby. For hiking trips, I'll arrange transport or meet you at a convenient starting point.

What if it rains?

Light rain = we go (it's part of the experience). Heavy rain = I'll suggest rescheduling or switching to indoor activities. Your safety comes first.

Can you help with other travel needs?

Happy to help with hotel recommendations, train bookings, and general China travel advice. Just ask!

Are your tours suitable for all fitness levels?

City tours: Yes, anyone can join (just expect stairs!). Hiking: Check the difficulty rating — I'll recommend the right trail for your fitness level.
