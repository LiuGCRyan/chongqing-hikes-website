WHAT TO EXPECT

🥾

Walking

Chongqing is a walking city. Expect 2-4 hours of walking with breaks. Wear comfortable shoes — this is a mountain city with stairs everywhere!

🌶️

Spicy Food

Chongqing cuisine is famous for being spicy and numbing (麻辣). I'll help you navigate the heat — or find milder options if you prefer.

📱

Content Ready

Every spot we visit is social-media worthy. I know the best angles, timing, and hidden spots for those perfect shots.

🌧️

Weather

Chongqing is called "Fog City" for a reason. Bring an umbrella in spring/summer, and dress in layers — it can be humid!
